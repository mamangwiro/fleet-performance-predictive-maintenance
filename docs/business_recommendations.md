# Business Recommendations

## 📌 Overview
Based on the predictive analysis of fleet performance and breakdown risk, the following recommendations are proposed to improve operational efficiency, reduce downtime, and enhance fleet reliability.

---

## 🚨 1. Prioritise High-Risk Vehicles for Maintenance
- Vehicles identified as high risk should be scheduled for **immediate inspection and servicing**
- Implement a **priority-based maintenance schedule** driven by model predictions

**Impact:**
- Reduces unexpected breakdowns  
- Minimises operational disruptions  

---

## 🔧 2. Implement Preventive Maintenance Strategy
- Shift from reactive maintenance to **predictive and preventive maintenance**
- Use model outputs to determine optimal servicing intervals

**Impact:**
- Extends vehicle lifespan  
- Reduces long-term maintenance costs  

---

## ⛽ 3. Monitor Fuel Efficiency as a Risk Indicator
- Vehicles with abnormal fuel consumption should be flagged for investigation
- Integrate fuel usage monitoring into maintenance decision-making

**Impact:**
- Early detection of mechanical issues  
- Improved cost control on fuel expenditure  

---

## 📦 4. Improve Delivery Performance Monitoring
- High rates of late deliveries may indicate underlying vehicle issues
- Combine operational KPIs with maintenance analytics

**Impact:**
- Enhances service reliability  
- Improves customer satisfaction  

---

## 📊 5. Integrate Dashboard into Operational Workflow
- Deploy the Power BI dashboard for **daily or weekly monitoring**
- Use it in management meetings for decision-making

**Impact:**
- Promotes data-driven culture  
- Enables faster response to emerging risks  

---

## 📈 6. Continuously Improve the Model
- Incorporate real-world fleet data over time
- Include additional variables such as:
  - Driver behaviour
  - Vehicle age
  - Sensor/telematics data

**Impact:**
- Improves prediction accuracy  
- Increases business value of the solution  

---

## ⚠️ 7. Establish Risk Thresholds and Alerts
- Define clear thresholds for:
  - High risk
  - Medium risk
- Set up alerts for vehicles exceeding thresholds

**Impact:**
- Enables proactive intervention  
- Prevents escalation of minor issues  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

By implementing these recommendations, organisations can transition from reactive fleet management to a proactive, data-driven approach. This will result in improved operational efficiency, reduced costs, and enhanced fleet reliability.
