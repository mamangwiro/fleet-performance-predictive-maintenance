# Dashboard Guide

## 📌 Overview
A Power BI dashboard was developed to visualise fleet performance and breakdown risk. The dashboard enables logistics managers to monitor key metrics, identify high-risk vehicles, and support proactive maintenance decisions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 🔹 Total Fleet Size
- Displays the total number of vehicles in the dataset
- Provides a quick overview of fleet scale

---

### 🔹 High-Risk Vehicles
- Shows the number of vehicles classified as high risk
- Helps prioritise maintenance actions

---

### 🔹 Risk Status Indicator
- Displays overall fleet risk condition (e.g., Controlled / At Risk)
- Acts as a quick health check for decision-makers

---

### 🔹 Fleet Risk Composition (Donut Chart)
- Breaks down the fleet into:
  - High Risk
  - Medium Risk
  - Low Risk
- Enables quick understanding of risk distribution across the fleet

---

### 🔹 Breakdown Probability Distribution (Histogram)
- Visualises the distribution of predicted breakdown probabilities
- Helps identify:
  - Concentration of high-risk vehicles
  - Spread of risk across the fleet

---

## 🎯 How to Use the Dashboard

### 1. Identify High-Risk Vehicles
- Focus on the "High Risk Vehicles" metric
- Prioritise these vehicles for inspection and maintenance

---

### 2. Monitor Fleet Health
- Use the Risk Status indicator to assess overall condition
- Track changes over time (if updated with new data)

---

### 3. Analyse Risk Distribution
- Use the donut chart to understand fleet segmentation
- Identify whether risk is concentrated or widespread

---

### 4. Investigate Breakdown Patterns
- Use the histogram to analyse probability trends
- Detect clusters of vehicles approaching high-risk thresholds

---

## 💼 Business Value

The dashboard provides:
- Real-time visibility into fleet risk exposure
- Data-driven support for maintenance planning
- Improved decision-making for logistics operations
- Ability to reduce downtime and operational disruptions

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The Power BI dashboard transforms model outputs into actionable insights, enabling logistics managers to move from reactive maintenance to a proactive, data-driven approach.
